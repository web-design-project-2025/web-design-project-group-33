Morbius 2022

Rating

Director Daniel Espinosa

Stars Jared Leto, Matt Smith, Adria Arjona

Morbius (2022) follows Dr. Michael Morbius (Jared Leto), a scientist who transforms into a living vampire while searching for a cure to a rare blood disease. The movie blends superhero action with horror elements but struggles to find a solid balance. Leto’s performance is intense but occasionally over the top, and the CGI effects are hit or miss.
